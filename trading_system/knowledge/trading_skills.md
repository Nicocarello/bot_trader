## 1. BASICS OF FINANCE (Corvinus University)

### Accounting & Balance Sheet

- Assets = Liabilities + Equity
- Equity = residual value after liabilities
- Double-entry bookkeeping ensures balance
- Economic events affect both sides of the balance sheet

### Money & Banking

- Functions of money:

  - Medium of exchange
  - Store of value
  - Unit of account
  - Standard of deferred payment
- Evolution:

  - Barter → commodity money → fiat money
- Banks:

  - Create money via lending
  - Manage liquidity and solvency risk

### Financial System

- Banks intermediating funds
- Securities markets:

  - Bonds, stocks, derivatives
- Balance of payments & FX markets
- Public finance and taxation

## 2. FINANCIAL INVESTMENTS & STOCK MARKETS

### Personal Finance Cycle

- Income → Consumption → Saving → Investment → Wealth

### Investment Types

### Risk & Return

- Higher risk → higher expected return

### Financial Markets

- Primary market: issuance
- Secondary market: trading
- Stocks = ownership
- Bonds = debt

### Market Efficiency

- Prices reflect available information

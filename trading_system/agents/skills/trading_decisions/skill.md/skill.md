# Trading Principles

## Core Philosophy

- Capital preservation > profit
- Only trade when edge is clear
- No trade is a valid decision

## Market Behavior

- Trending market → favor momentum
- Ranging market → favor mean reversion
- High volatility → reduce size or avoid trading

## Entry Rules

- Enter only when:
  - multiple signals align
  - context supports the trade
  - risk/reward > 2:1

## Exit Rules

- Always define stop-loss
- Never average down blindly
- Take profit when edge disappears

## No-Trade Conditions

- conflicting signals
- weak news context
- low confidence (< threshold)
- abnormal volatility spikes

## Risk Management

- never risk > X% per trade
- reduce size when uncertainty is high
- stop trading after drawdown threshold

## Behavioral Discipline

- do not overtrade
- avoid reacting to noise
- prefer fewer, higher-quality trades
